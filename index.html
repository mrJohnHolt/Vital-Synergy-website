What We Do

At Vital Synergy, we support organisations at every stage of their energy journey.

Strategy & Feasibility

We assess project viability, conduct audits and secure funding,identifying carbon-saving opportunities, renewable potential and financial pathways to support decarbonisation, renewable generation and long-term energy goals.

Design, Planning & Delivery

We design and deliver compliant, buildable energy systems,from concept to planning, CDM compliance, and project management,ensuring safe, efficient and timely implementation aligned with your strategy.

Utilities, Connections & Procurement

We manage grid and utility connections, de-risk critical interfaces, and support energy procurement,securing contracts that balance cost, flexibility, sustainability and long-term energy resilience.

Advisory & Ongoing Support

We provide ESG and energy management advisory, helping track emissions, meet compliance, optimise consumption and align projects with investor, regulatory and sustainability goals for ongoing performance and carbon reduction.
