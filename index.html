What We Do

At Vital Synergy, we support your estate at every stage, from concept and design to installation, commissioning and lifecycle care.

Mechanical Services

Plumbing, ventilation, cooling, heating, sprinklers and gas & oil systems, engineered for safety, efficiency and long-term operational performance across every building type.

Low-Carbon & Renewables

Heat pumps, solar PV, hydrogen-ready infrastructure and decarbonisation retrofits that cut emissions and help organisations futureproof their estates without compromising performance.

Electrical & Critical Systems

Lighting and power, security and data, data centres and BMS controls, intelligent, resilient infrastructure that keeps critical environments running with maximum uptime.

Design & Lifecycle Support

In-house design and consultancy plus planned and reactive maintenance, coordinated, engineering-led support that keeps your systems compliant and performing for the long term.
