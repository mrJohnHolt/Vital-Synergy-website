Who We Are

Bespoke mechanical & energy systems for complex buildings

Vital Synergy designs, installs and maintains bespoke mechanical and energy systems for schools, factories, offices, hotels and other large buildings. Our work spans advanced heating and cooling, full energy centres, and Building Management System integration, built for performance, reliability and long-term energy efficiency.

Every solution is engineered to reduce running costs, cut carbon and improve building performance over the lifetime of the asset.

Our Journey

From Vital Heating Solutions to Vital Synergy

The business began as Vital Heating Solutions, a contractor built on straightforward values: honest advice, reliable workmanship, and installations done properly. From the outset, the focus was on building trust with clients through consistent delivery rather than volume.

As the team grew and the scope of work expanded into more technically complex projects, including energy centres, BMS integration and large-scale low-carbon systems, the business matured to match. The rebrand to Vital Synergy reflects that evolution: a broader capability, a larger team, and a sharper focus on the commercial and industrial sectors where building performance is critical.

The values that defined the original business remain unchanged. Vital Synergy continues to operate as a specialist rather than a generalist, taking on projects where engineering rigour and clear communication make a tangible difference to outcomes.
