Low-Carbon Heating

Efficient, future-ready heat pump solutions

Air source heat pumps draw warmth from the outside air to provide reliable heating and hot water for commercial premises, even in cold weather. They run on electricity rather than fossil fuels, making them a cleaner and more efficient choice for businesses looking to reduce running costs and meet their net zero goals.

At Vital Synergy, we design every system around your building, your usage and your budget. From the first site survey through to installation and aftercare, our team handles the detail so you get a heat pump solution that performs year after year.
