Ground Source Technology

Sustainable heat from the ground up

Ground source heat pumps capture natural warmth stored in the earth and use it to heat your commercial premises and water. Because ground temperatures stay steady through every season, these systems run at consistently high efficiency, offering some of the lowest running costs and carbon emissions of any heating technology.

At Vital Synergy, we assess your ground conditions, energy demand and building layout to design a system that is right for your site. From geological survey and ground loop design through to installation and ongoing support, our team manages the whole process so your investment delivers reliable performance for decades to come.
